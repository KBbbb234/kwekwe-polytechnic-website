Our History

Kwekwe Polytechnic has a rich history of academic excellence and community service. Founded to provide high-quality technical and vocational education, it has grown into one of Zimbabwe's leading polytechnic institutions.

Over the years, the institution has nurtured thousands of skilled graduates who have contributed to national and regional development in engineering, business, and technology sectors.

Our institution continues to evolve, embracing technology and Innovation while maintaining a strong focus on hands-on learning. We aim to prepare our students for both local and international opportunities through quality education, research, and community engagement.

Our Mission

To provide relevant and high-quality technical education and training that equips students with practical skills, innovation, and entrepreneurship to meet global industry demands.
